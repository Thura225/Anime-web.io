Description

The anime series that adapted from a manga called Shingeki no Kyoujin written by Hajime Isayama.


Eren Yeager(MC) is the boy living behind the great wall at Paradis Island with his family and his two friends called Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlet peacefully.But everyting turned to collapse when the monster called Titan appeared in front of the wall,destroying buildings and killing people including Eren's mother.After that,Eren desired to kill all the titans and join the military.You can see the adventure of Eren and his friends and the secrets behind the titans at the video below.
